About

  • Director: Aamir Khan
  • Producer: Aamir Khan Productions
  • Budget: ₹12 crore
  • Box Office: ₹135 crore worldwide

    • Taare Zameen Par (English: Like Stars on Earth) is a 2007 Indian drama film directed by Aamir Khan and written by Amole Gupte. The story follows Ishaan Awasthi, an 8-year-old boy whose world is filled with wonders and creativity, but who struggles academically due to undiagnosed dyslexia. Misunderstood by his parents and teachers, Ishaan is sent to a boarding school where his self-esteem plummets. His life takes a turn when a new art teacher, Ram Shankar Nikumbh (played by Aamir Khan), recognizes his struggles and helps him overcome his learning difficulties through patience, empathy, and creative teaching methods.

    The film is praised for its sensitive portrayal of learning disabilities, the pressures of the education system, and the importance of understanding a child’s individual strengths. It combines heartfelt storytelling with soulful music composed by Shankar–Ehsaan–Loy, making it both emotionally moving and socially impactful.
